Our Academic Structure

Primary Section (Std. 1 to 5)

Government-aided curriculum with a focus on foundational learning, moral education, and holistic growth under the State Board guidelines.

Secondary Section (Std. 6 to 10)

Privately managed classes offering modern teaching methods, English & Marathi medium, and optional computer education.

Higher Secondary (Std. 11 & 12)

Private streams in Science and Commerce. Includes labs, project-based learning, NEET/JEE & CA Foundation coaching options.

About Nutan Vidhyamandir

Established in 1985, Nutan Vidhyamandir is committed to delivering accessible and quality education to all. Our unique hybrid model combines Government education for primary classes and private schooling for secondary and higher secondary levels.

Our goal is to equip every student—from rural or urban background—with a strong academic foundation, practical skills, and moral values.
