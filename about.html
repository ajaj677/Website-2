Nutan Vidya Mandir

Deolali Camp, Bhagur, Nashik – Maharashtra State Board

Our Journey

Nutan Vidya Mandir, located in Bhagur near Deolali Camp, Nashik, was established in 1997 and is managed as a private-aided, co‑educational institution serving students from Class 1 through 4 (primary) and Classes 5–12.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to provide accessible, values-driven education grounded in the Marathi medium while gradually integrating modern teaching methodologies. We aim to build confident, responsible youth ready to adapt in a changing world.

Academic & Infrastructure

  • Marathi is the primary medium of instruction, aligning with local needs and language culture.
  • The school operates in permanent buildings with four well-maintained classrooms and dedicated staff rooms.
  • Infrastructure includes separate boys' and girls' toilets, accessible ramps, library with ~245 books, playground and mid-day meal facilities.

Community Engagement

Nutan Vidya Mandir serves the local urban community of Bhagur and Deolali Camp, offering education grounded in cultural values and social responsibility. Many generations of local families have been educated here.
